About Me

A sketchbook obsession that became a business.

Hi, I'm Mugundan — a self-taught artist from Tamil Nadu, India who's been drawing since I could hold a pencil. What started as doodles in school notebooks grew into a full-blown obsession with light, shadow, and texture.

I specialise in pencil shading, watercolour portraits, and charcoal landscapes. Every commission is drawn entirely by hand — no digital shortcuts, just paper, patience, and a lot of love.

Whether it's a portrait of your loved one, your pet, your home, or a scene from your imagination — I'll pour everything into making it special.
